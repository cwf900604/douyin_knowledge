### About Us

Yuanyu Drive is a Shenzhen-based manufacturer specializing in **high-performance servo motors** for RC hobby, industrial automation, robotics, and UAV applications. We operate two distinct brands:

- **GX Precision** — Competition-grade servo engineering for professional RC, robotics, and industrial applications
- **YP Motion** — Professional performance at practical pricing for wholesale, education, and maker channels

We serve B2B buyers worldwide: RC hobby distributors, drone/UAV parts suppliers, robotics integrators, industrial automation companies, and cross-border ecommerce sellers.
